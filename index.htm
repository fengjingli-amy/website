About Me

Welcome to my website!

My name is Jingli Feng, and I am a forth-year Ph.D. Candidate in Accounting at NUS Business School, National University of Singapore. You can download my CV here.

Research Interest

Managerial Accounting, Auditing, Human Resource Management, Applied Machine Learning

　

Research Statement

I am particularly committed to conducting impactful accounting research on internal and external labor markets, with the goal of advancing our understanding of how individual characteristics, organizational designs including performance measurement systems, and institutional factors such as policy development influence employee career trajectories both within and beyond organizational boundaries.
